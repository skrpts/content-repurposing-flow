## Platforms
- LinkedIn: professional tone, 1300 char limit, 3-5 hashtags
- X/Twitter: concise, 280 chars, 1-2 hashtags
- Instagram: visual-first, 2200 char caption, 20-30 hashtags
- Threads: conversational, 500 chars, minimal hashtags
- Newsletter: long-form, personal tone, clear CTA
